Initial release of Podcasto, a full-featured podcast player.

- Search & subscribe to podcasts via iTunes
- Audio player with skip 10s/30s, playlist with drag-to-reorder
- Download episodes for offline listening
- Tags, bookmarks, played/unplayed filters
- AI-powered discovery with Gemini
- Built-in web server for remote control
- Tunnel access with password protection
- Local & Google Drive backup
- Listening history & volume normalization
- 4 languages: EN, FR, DE, ES
- Material 3 design
